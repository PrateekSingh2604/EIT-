“ The Social club promotes community engagement, social responsibility, and student networking. ”

The Social Club at Echelon Institute of Technology is a vibrant community comprising various sub-clubs that cater to diverse interests.
The Social Media and Photography Club focuses on digital storytelling, capturing significant moments, and fostering creative expression.
Raso Vaisa (The Food Club) celebrates culinary arts, offering students a platform to explore cooking and discover new cuisines.
The Last Centre provides a relaxing space for stress relief and promotes mental well-being.
The Marketing Club delves into the world of branding, advertising, and strategic communication, helping members develop essential marketing skills.
Samatva (NGO) Club is dedicated to social service and community outreach, encouraging students to engage in meaningful social initiatives and promote equality.
Together, these sub-clubs create a dynamic environment for students to explore their passions, develop new skills, and contribute to the community.
